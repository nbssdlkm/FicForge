# Responsible Use

## What FicForge Is

FicForge is a local AI-assisted writing tool for fanfiction creators who want to write their own stories.
It is not a replacement for human creativity.

## Data & Privacy

- **All data stays on your computer.** FicForge does not upload your data to any server controlled by FicForge. When you use AI writing features, your content is sent to the API provider you configure (e.g. DeepSeek) — this is necessary for AI generation and is under your control.
- **Your writing is never used to train any AI model.** FicForge calls the API you configure (e.g. DeepSeek). Many major AI providers state in their API terms of service that API data is not used to train their models (as distinct from their consumer-facing chat products). Please refer to your chosen provider's privacy policy for details. FicForge itself does not collect, store, or transmit your data.
- **Built-in semantic search runs entirely on your machine.** No cloud services involved.

## Content Sources

- **Only import content you created or have legal rights to use.**
- The import feature is designed for migrating your own existing work, not for importing others' writing.
- Because FicForge strictly enforces local operation and absolute privacy, the system has no access or ability to scan or verify the source of files on your local device. Therefore, the user bears sole responsibility for ensuring they have legal rights to the imported content.

## Publishing & Attribution

- If you publish AI-assisted content on any platform, **we recommend disclosing AI involvement.**
- Respect the rules of your creative community. Many fanfiction communities have specific policies regarding AI-assisted content — please follow them.
- FicForge's export feature includes an optional AI attribution notice, enabled by default.

## Our Position

FicForge provides tools for people who can't find the stories they want to read and choose to create their own.
We respect the labor and rights of all creators, regardless of their tools.
We oppose plagiarism, misrepresentation of AI-generated content as fully human-written, and unauthorized use of others' work in any form.

## Why Open Source

We chose the AGPLv3 license to ensure FicForge remains an open, transparent tool that cannot be closed-sourced for commercial exploitation. We reject the black-boxing of AI technology, and we reject others profiting opaquely from this project's open-source work.
